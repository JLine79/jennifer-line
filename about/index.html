Why 'Pink Sea Projects'?

Once upon a time I worked on the client side of web support. We asked for pink icons and got a pink sea... It inspired me to learn some new skills and use my old ones to provide a service that is based on listening and understanding your needs. You want a pink sea? You got it! You don't? No worries, I will work with you carefully and thoughtfully to make sure we understand each other.

I also offer support with planning, group work, process design and analysis. If you want to dissect and improve the way you work, I can help to plan and run sessions to gather and combine the perspectives of your team for a clearer picture; or create a meaningful interaction with stakeholders to shape your plans. If you need it, I can produce a decision report for you, crunch some numbers and give you something useful in plain English for your next steps.
